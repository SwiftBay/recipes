Ingredients:
 - 1 derica of garri
 - 1 litre of water

Directions:
  1. Boil the water to 100 degrees
  2. Pour the water into a bowl
  3.Gently and steadily pour the garri to be at par level with the water
  4.Grab your turning stick and stir continuously until the mix is thickened
  5.Serve while still hot
